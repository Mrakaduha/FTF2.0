Fuck The Fear

A small project about fear, breath, and spaces between.

Lifetime

Throughout life, you never stay the same even two consecutive days. Your body replaces itself in countless ways, only two things stay with you from birth to death: your fear and your breath. And so long as you remain in the current of life, the current decides for you. If you want to choose for yourself you must look for gaps — for only in a gap can you set a direction of your own, before the current claims you again.

This project is meant as a collection of gaps, things that help you for a moment leave the current. For that matter however, you can also follow water. It always finds a gap.

More water to follow if you can't or don't want to get out.
